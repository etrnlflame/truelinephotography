Hello! My name is Melanie Leon. I've been in love with art and horses for so long, it's a wonder I didn't figure out how to combine them sooner.

As new as 2018 I've been training in photography. It all started with a passion for action shots at horse jumping competitions. If I could choose between competing on my own horse versus spending the entire competition taking pictures, it would be a tough decision.
​

​All of the moments excite me, from that perfect shot as the horse goes over the jump, to the fine detail that captures a horse's personalty or intimate moments. Photography isn't a career to me, or even an income. It's a dream to capture the right picture that pet owners will keep for the rest of their lives.
​
In addition to horses, I've been practicing photography with her pawed-furbabbies and would love to take pictures of yours too!
